How To Use This Tool
  • Input the tech levels you already have as numbers, in the boxes at the top.
  • The list below the boxes will dynamically update to tell you which items will increase tech levels. Some items will be recommended by guides (orange) or give multiple tech levels (green) or both.
  • When you've found an item you can make/use, make it, deconstruct it, then hit 'apply' next to it in the list. Your tech levels (in the boxes on the page) will be updated, and the list will update as well, listing the new items to further increase your levels.
  • To go from tech 5 to tech 7 in a tech, use the items listed in the "5 -> 7 Upgrades" notes at the top, next to the input boxes. The dynamic list isn't usually useful for going from tech 5 to 7.
